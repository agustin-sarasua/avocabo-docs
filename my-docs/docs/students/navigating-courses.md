# Navigating Courses

This guide explains how to browse your courses, understand their structure, and find the content you need.

## Accessing Your Courses

### From the Home Screen

1. See your courses in the **Course Progress** section
2. Tap any course card to open it
3. Or tap **View All** to see all courses

### From the Courses Tab

1. Tap **Courses** in the bottom navigation
2. See all your enrolled courses
3. Tap any course to open it

---

## Course List

### What You'll See

Each course shows:
- **Course Image** - Visual identifier
- **Course Title** - Name of the course
- **Progress Bar** - How much you've completed
- **Status** - Active or completed

### Sorting and Filtering

If you have many courses:
- Most recent activity first
- In-progress courses highlighted
- Completed courses marked

---

## Inside a Course

### Course Details Screen

When you open a course, you'll see:

1. **Course Header**
   - Title and description
   - Course image
   - Overall progress

2. **Module List**
   - All modules in order
   - Progress per module
   - Tap to expand

3. **Lessons**
   - Listed within each module
   - Status indicators
   - Tap to start

## Starting a Lesson

### To Begin

1. Find the lesson you want
2. Tap on it
3. The lesson screen opens
4. Start with the first exercise

### Continuing a Lesson

If you previously started:
1. Tap the lesson
2. You'll resume where you left off
3. Progress is saved automatically

### Reviewing Completed Lessons

Even after completing:
1. Tap the lesson
2. Review exercises and answers
3. See your scores

---

## Course Progress

### Progress Bar

The progress bar shows:
- **Green** portion = completed lessons
- **Remaining** portion = lessons to go
- **Percentage** displayed

### Module Progress

Each module has its own progress:
- Shows lessons completed in that module
- Helps you track where you are
- Indicates next steps

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See a Course

Check:
- You're signed in to the correct account
- The course is assigned to you
- Ask your teacher to verify access

### Lessons Are Locked

This is normal:
- Teachers release content gradually
- Check back later for new lessons
- Contact your teacher if urgent

### Progress Not Showing

Try:
- Refresh the app
- Check internet connection
- Wait a moment and try again
