## Getting Started

### Accessing the App

The student app is available on:
- **Web**: [student.PragmaTeach.com](https://student.PragmaTeach.com)
- **iOS**: App Store (coming soon)
- **Android**: Google Play (coming soon)

### Signing In

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

If you don't have an account, your teacher or administrator will create one for you.

### First Login

On your first login:
1. You may need to verify your email
2. Set a new password if prompted
3. Complete any required profile setup
