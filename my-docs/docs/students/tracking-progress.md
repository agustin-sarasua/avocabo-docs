# Tracking Your Progress

PragmaTeach helps you track your learning progress so you can see how far you've come and what's next. This guide shows you how to use the progress features.

## Progress Overview

### Accessing Progress

1. Tap **Progress** in the bottom navigation
2. Or tap the Progress tab on the Home screen
3. See your complete learning overview

### What You'll See

- **Overall Completion** - Percentage of all content finished
- **Course Breakdown** - Progress per course
- **Weekly Activity** - Your learning this week
- **Recent Lessons** - What you've done recently

## Scores and Performance

### Understanding Your Scores

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **90-100%** | Excellent! Great understanding |
| **70-89%** | Good work! Minor review needed |
| **50-69%** | Okay, but practice more |
| **Below 50%** | Need to study this topic more |

### Score Details

For each lesson:
- Overall lesson score
- Exercises correct/total
- Areas of strength
- Areas to improve

### Improving Scores

If your score is low:
1. Review the lesson content
2. Understand incorrect answers
3. Retry if attempts available
4. Ask your teacher for help

## Tips for Better Progress

### Building Consistency

| Strategy | Why It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| Study at same time daily | Builds habit |
| Complete one lesson per day | Consistent progress |
| Don't skip weekends entirely | Maintains momentum |
| Review previous lessons | Reinforces learning |

### When You're Struggling

1. **Slow down** - Take more time per lesson
2. **Review** - Go back to earlier content
3. **Ask for help** - Use AI assistant or teacher
4. **Take breaks** - Rest helps learning

### When You're Doing Great

1. **Keep going** - Don't lose momentum
2. **Challenge yourself** - Try harder exercises
3. **Help others** - Explaining helps you learn
4. **Stay consistent** - Success comes from routine

## Troubleshooting

### Progress Not Updating

- Check internet connection
- Refresh the app
- Wait a moment and check again
- Progress saves automatically

### Missing Completed Lesson

- Verify you submitted final answer
- Check the lesson wasn't interrupted
- Contact teacher if issue persists

### Score Seems Wrong

- Review your answers
- Check if partial credit applies
- Note that some exercises are weighted
